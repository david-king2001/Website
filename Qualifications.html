Experience

Over the past years, I have amassed a great level of job experience both in entry-level and more experienced positions. I started like most high schoolers did, working at a local fast-food restaurant. I spent the latter half of my senior year of high school working until I left for college. After beginning my college career, I was offered a position working for an Accounting company on the technical side of the business. I was able to work from home, completing various reports and creating spreadsheets for use with clients and internal tracking. The summer before my Sophomore year, I received a paid internship offer to work for AFCEC/CZCA, a civil engineering division within the Air force.
I worked with a small team based at Lackland AFB. At the time, the pandemic had just begun and we were forced to work in a purely virtual capacity. My role was to provide support in a general capacity. I worked on a range of projects such as; preparing leadership briefing, data analysis, and review of various contracts and/or technical documents. This position served to provide an introduction to the formal workplace, providing experience I plan to use in the future.
